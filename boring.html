About

I combine professional level skills in GIS - from ESRI products to open source Python libraries - and front end web development with a research focus on drinking water. My work experience in these areas spans from helping the Florida Geologic Survey (FGS) transition their geologic map data to federal GeMS standards, to supporting the Engineering team at Florida Rural Water Association (FRWA), to building web apps using Vue.js on a team of front end developers at ASCAP.

My masters research in Geography focuses on drinking water infrastructure, urban development and "creative extraction," especially in urban New Jersey. My thesis brought a mixed-methods approach to study the drinking water lead contamination crisis in Newark, NJ.

Currently based in New York, I am consulting for FGS and pursuing opportunities in drinking water and related fields.
